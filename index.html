How Deployment Works

01

Submit Your Docker Image URL

Send a standard image reference such as docker.io/your-org/your-app:tag.

02

We Deploy It

We run and expose your containerized service on the hosting platform.

03

Track Energy in Real Time

Monitor usage through API endpoints and from inside Linux via /proc.

Energy Visibility with procpower

Every deployed workload can be analyzed for power draw. Query the API for dashboarding, or read directly from the procfs interface. 


cat /proc/energy/cgroup

Your app can also parse these files directly to surface energy usage within your own product UI.

Pricing Depends on Usage

Billing is usage-based: customers pay according to measured energy and infrastructure consumption.

Monthly Price = Base Platform Fee + (Measured kWh × Energy Rate × Infrastructure Factor)
